Guest FAQ

Everything you need to know

Are kids welcome at the wedding?

We love your little ones! However, to allow all guests (including parents) an evening of relaxation and uninhibited revelry, we respectfully ask that the Sangeet and Reception be adults-only. Children are more than welcome at the Mehndi ceremony.

Can I bring a plus one?

Due to venue capacity, we can only accommodate guests who are formally named on your digital invitation. We appreciate your understanding and can't wait to celebrate with you!

What will the weather be like?

February brings beautiful, mild winter weather. Expect pleasant, sunny days around 22°C (72°F) and chilly evenings dropping to 12°C (54°F). We recommend bringing a shawl or light jacket for the evening outdoor events.
